# Professional Email Template

Create polished, effective professional emails for any business scenario. This template helps you generate well-structured emails that maintain a professional tone while clearly communicating your message.

<PromptTemplate>
  Write a professional email about {topic} to {recipient}. 
  
  The tone should be {tone: professional, friendly, formal, assertive} and the purpose is to {purpose: inform, request, follow up, introduce, propose}.
  
  Include appropriate greeting and sign-off. Keep the email concise and focused on the main message.
</PromptTemplate>

## Example Outputs

<LlmSampleTabs>
  <LlmSample slug="chatgpt">
    Subject: Proposal for Q3 Marketing Campaign Collaboration
    
    Dear Ms. Reynolds,
    
    I hope this email finds you well. I'm reaching out regarding a potential collaboration opportunity between our companies for the upcoming Q3 marketing initiatives.
    
    Based on our recent market analysis, we've identified several areas where our combined expertise could create significant value for both organizations. I'd like to propose a brief meeting to discuss how we might align our efforts to maximize impact across both our customer bases.
    
    Would you be available for a 30-minute call next Tuesday or Wednesday afternoon? I'd be happy to send a calendar invitation with further details.
    
    Thank you for considering this proposal. I look forward to potentially working together.
    
    Best regards,
    
    Alex Morgan
    Marketing Director
    Innovate Solutions
  </LlmSample>
