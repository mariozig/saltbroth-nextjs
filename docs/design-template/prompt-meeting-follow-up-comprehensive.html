Prompt Body

This carefully crafted prompt consistently generates professional, well-structured follow-up emails. Copy and customize it for your needs:

Generate a professional follow-up email for a Q1 Performance Review meeting with the following details:

Meeting Date: March 1, 2024
Duration: 60 minutes
Location: Virtual Conference Room A

Attendees:
- John Smith (Sales)
- Sarah Johnson (Marketing)
- Mike Chen (Product)

Key Discussion Points:
1. Q1 Sales Performance
2. Marketing Campaign Results
3. Product Roadmap Updates

Action Items Required:
1. John: Prepare Q2 sales forecast (Due: March 15)
2. Sarah: Complete campaign analytics report (Due: March 8)
3. Mike: Schedule product feature demo (Due: March 10)

Next Meeting: April 1, 2024

Additional Notes:
- Keep the tone professional but clear
- Include all key discussion points
- List action items with owners and deadlines
- Mention the next meeting date
