Prompt Guide & Usage

Meeting follow-up emails are essential for documenting discussions, clarifying responsibilities, and maintaining accountability. This prompt template helps you generate professional, well-structured follow-ups with minimal effort.

Use this template to create comprehensive meeting summaries that include all key discussion points, action items with clear ownership, and next steps. Your follow-up emails will maintain a professional tone while ensuring all participants are aligned on outcomes.

Works with various meeting types including project discussions, team syncs, and client consultations. Simply customize the bracketed sections with your specific meeting details.

The Prompt

Just copy and paste this prompt into any AI system. Customize the [bracketed] sections with your meeting details. 

I need you to create a professional follow-up email summarizing a meeting that took place. 

Meeting Details:
Title: [Meeting Title]
Date: [Date]
Duration: [Duration]
Location: [Location]
Attendees: [List of attendees with roles]

Key Discussion Points:
[Bullet points of main topics discussed]

Action Items:
[List of action items with owners and deadlines]

Next Steps:
[Any planned follow-up meetings or future milestones]

Please include:
- A brief introduction thanking attendees
- Clear section headings
- All action items with owners and due dates
- Next steps and timeline
- A professional sign-off
- Format the email for easy scanning
